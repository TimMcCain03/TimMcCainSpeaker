About Tim

Born in Southern California in 1973 and raised in Sacramento, Tim McCain's life took a dramatic turn at age 13 when he became homeless. At 19, he began an 8-year prison sentence that marked the start of a long journey of transformation. After his release, he met his future wife, started a family, and built a new life from the ground up.

Tim joined PhotoLynx as a sales rep and rose to become its CEO, later leading ImageQuix as President. A visionary leader and ordained pastor, he now serves Freedom Life Church in Hampton, VA. Tim speaks to diverse audiences about resilience, leadership, and the power of faith-driven transformation.
