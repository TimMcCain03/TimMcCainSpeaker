About Tim McCain

Born in Southern California and raised in Sacramento, Tim faced hardship in his early life including homelessness and addiction. At age nineteen, he ws sentenced to eight years in a state prision, marking the beginning of a transformational journy. Following his release, he met his wife, started a family, and built a new life from the ground up. A visionary leader and ordained pastor, he now serves his community with Freedom Life Church in Hampton, VA. Tim now speaks to diverse audiences about motivation, leadership, and the power of faith driven transformation.
